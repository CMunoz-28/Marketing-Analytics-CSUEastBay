## Project Overview

This project presents a **conceptual product and brand development case** for *SnackPassport*, combining **consumer survey research** with **product interface design** to support data-driven product and branding decisions.

The objective was to evaluate consumer preferences, identify brand positioning opportunities, and design a **conceptual product experience** aligned with target customer needs.

---

## Project Objectives

- Analyze consumer survey data to understand preferences and behaviors  
- Identify product and brand positioning opportunities  
- Translate research insights into a conceptual product design  
- Align product features with brand strategy and customer expectations  

---

## Deliverables

- **Figma Product Mockup**  
  Conceptual interface design illustrating proposed product features and user experience.

- **Survey Analysis (Excel)**  
  Quantitative analysis of survey responses used to inform product and brand decisions.
