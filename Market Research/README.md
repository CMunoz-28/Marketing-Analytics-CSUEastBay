# Market Research

**Consumer Perception, Brand Extension Viability & Resale Program Adoption**

**Course:** Market Research  
**Program:** Master of Science in Marketing Analytics  
**Institution:** California State University, East Bay  

---

# Project Overview

This project presents a **primary market research study evaluating consumer perceptions of AthleticCo., a premium activewear brand**.  

The research focuses on four strategic business questions:

- How consumers perceive the brand today
- Whether the brand can successfully extend into **menswear and footwear**
- Consumer openness to a **brand-managed resale program**
- Which **marketing channels drive product discovery**

The study integrates **survey research, statistical analysis, and strategic marketing interpretation** to provide actionable recommendations for brand growth.

Data was collected using **Qualtrics surveys** and analyzed using **IBM SPSS Statistics and Microsoft Excel**.

---

# Research Objectives

The primary goals of this study were to:

* Evaluate **brand perception, trust, and satisfaction**
* Measure **Net Promoter Score (NPS)** and customer advocacy
* Assess **brand extension potential** for menswear and footwear
* Evaluate consumer openness to a **resale / recommerce program**
* Identify **top marketing discovery channels**
* Translate insights into **strategic marketing recommendations**

# Key Insights & Findings

Key findings from the research include:

* **Brand Trust:** Moderate-high consumer trust (Mean = 3.61 / 5)
* **Brand Satisfaction:** Positive overall satisfaction (Mean = 7.04 / 10)
* **Net Promoter Score:** **–16.7**, indicating a loyalty gap between promoters and detractors
* **Primary Brand Association:** Women's apparel (77% of respondents)
* **Brand Extension Interest:**

  - Menswear: Moderate interest (Mean = 2.94 / 5)
  - Footwear: Lowest extension interest (Mean = 2.78 / 5)

* **Resale Program Interest:** Slightly positive consumer openness (Mean = 3.13 / 5)

---

# Marketing Channel Insights

Top product discovery channels identified by respondents:

1. **Social Media**
2. **Influencer Marketing**
3. **Online Advertising / Search**
4. **In-Store Discovery**

These results indicate a **digital-first marketing strategy** is critical for brand awareness and engagement.

---

# Strategic Marketing Recommendations

Based on the research findings, the following strategic priorities were identified:

### 1. Address the Loyalty Gap

With an NPS of **–16.7**, the brand should prioritize:

* Customer experience improvements
* Post-purchase engagement programs
* Advocacy and loyalty initiatives

---

### 2. Selective Brand Extension Strategy

* Begin with **menswear expansion**
* Delay **footwear launch** until brand credibility strengthens

---

### 3. Pilot a Resale Program

Consumers expressed openness to resale programs if trust mechanisms are included:

* Buyback programs
* Sanitation certification
* Condition transparency
* Risk-free returns

---

### 4. Invest in Digital Marketing

Focus marketing investment on:

* Social media platforms
* Influencer partnerships
* Targeted digital advertising
